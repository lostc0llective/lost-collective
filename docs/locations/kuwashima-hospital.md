## Role in wider context

- **Part of the Yubari post-coal story.** The hospital sits within the broader urban contraction of Yubari, which saw a collapse from over 100,000 residents in the 1960s to under 10,000 today. The hospital closure is a symptom of the wider demographic collapse following Hokutan's bankruptcy and the end of large-scale coal mining. [S1]
- **Adjacent Lost Collective work.** Brett's "Streetscapes of Yubari" series and the Shimizusawa Thermal Power Plant photography document the same city in the same period.
